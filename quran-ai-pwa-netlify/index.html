بَيَان
Your Quranic AI Companion
"Bayyaan" — Arabic word for clarity, eloquence, and explanation.
That is the promise.
Bayyaan is not a general chatbot. It is a specialized Quranic companion trained to sit beside you as you read, bringing clarity to every ayah, every story, every divine command. Its purpose is singular: to bring you closer to the Book of Allah.
✦ ﷽ ✦
Why You Can Trust Bayyaan

Bayyaan Only Speaks from the Quran

Bayyaan doesn't browse the internet or guess. It only looks at the Quran, authentic tafsir, and the specific verse you're reading — never random websites or unverified opinions.

Rooted in Classical Scholarship

Bayyaan's explanations reflect the methodology of classical scholars — structured to provide clear meaning, followed by contextual tafsir rooted in the tradition of the great mufassirun. No personal speculation. No invented rulings.

A Guide, Never a Judge

Bayyaan will never declare something haram or halal on its own authority. It explains what the Quran says, what scholars have derived, and always encourages you to consult learned scholars for binding rulings. This boundary is sacred.

Analytical, Not Creative

Bayyaan does not invent religious claims. When you ask about a Prophet's story, it draws directly from the Quran's own narrative. Its reliability comes from being tightly coupled to the divine text — not from generating new religious ideas.

You Are Always the Final Check

The Arabic of the Quran is displayed before you at all times. Bayyaan explains — you reflect. For matters of importance, your own heart, guided by sincerity, and consultation with knowledgeable scholars always takes precedence.

Bayyaan is a bridge, not a destination. It bridges the gap between you and the profound meanings of the Quran — a starting point for deeper understanding, a companion for reflection. Trust it as a well-informed guide. And always remember: the Quran itself is the ultimate source.
